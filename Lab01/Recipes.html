Sweet Pork

Ingrediants

Instructions
  • 4 cups of pork belly, sliced
  • 7 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 3-5 pieces of star anise
  • 1 tbsp galirc, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar, packed tight water, lots of it
  • additional soy sauce for browning eggs
  • Marinate the pork belly in soy sauce and oyster sauce. Set aside.
  • Boil the eggs. Then remove the eggshells when done
  • In a large pot, add oil, add garlic and ginger. Stir until brown. Add brown sugar.
  • When the sugar is melted, add the pork belly marinaged including juices and all.
  • Stir for a few minutes until the pork gets slightly white. Then add 4 cupds of water or more until the pork is all covered.
  • Cook for 1 hour or more. For more tender pork, keep adding water and cook 2 hours or longer. You can add the star anise at any time
  • The water will evaporate. When it reaches the thickness you like, remove from head and serve
