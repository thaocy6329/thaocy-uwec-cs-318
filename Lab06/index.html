What We Do

A unique fishing rod personally designed by you! You are able to determine the Rod type (spinning or casting), Length, Action, Handle type (split-grip or normal), Color, and Design! At Erie Custom Rods we take clients' orders and create their rods for them. We only use high quality blanks, guides, handles, and threads.

The Building Process

We start out by finding the spine of a high quality blank, once this is done we mark where the handle ends so we know where to put our design. We then spend hours delicately weaving threads around the blank to create your special design. Once the design is done it is time to tie on the guides. The guides are hand picked to match your style and length of rod, you can even choose the color of thread that the guides are tied on with. Once the guides are tied on, we mount the handle on the rod and glue it in place. While the glue is drying we epoxy the design and the guide wraps, we then place it in a rod dryer to sit for 24-48 hours. Once the rod is dry it's ready to fish.
